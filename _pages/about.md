Hi, I'm Shao Wang, a 2nd-year Master's student at [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://en.sjtu.edu.cn/).

My research interests lie in building efficient and large-scale systems for emerging ML workloads like LLMs and AI agents. Now, I work as a research assistant at CMU [Catalyst Lab](https://catalyst.cs.cmu.edu/), advised by Prof. [Zhihao Jia](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~zhihaoj2/).

Beyond research, I'm an open-source enthusiast, having maintained MLSys projects like [Kubeflow](https://github.com/kubeflow) for several years. My open-source journey began in my undergraduate years at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I also obtained my bachelor's degree.

I plan to apply for CS Ph.D. programs in North America starting in Fall 2027. Please feel free to drop me an email if you would like to connect!
